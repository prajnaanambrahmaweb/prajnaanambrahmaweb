The organization aims at imparting Sanathana Dharma (understanding the tendency of universe and merging with it) to seekers (or anyone who is interested in exploring his/her self) in a joyful atmosphere.

It is a tragedy that most people think that yoga is just about postures and spirituality has become a fashion, this attitude is not going to help much. Our Guruji is concerned with this trend and wants to provide authentic teachings to everyone. The doors are open!! come, be a part of Guru's family (Gurukulam), let's celebrate and learn.

Disciple: Oh wise and all knowing one, take me to the realm of perfect peace.
Master: If I take you to that realm, it will no longer be peaceful.
